Stephen Hawking

Stephen Hawking was a renowned theoretical physicist, cosmologist, and author. Born on January 8, 1942, he made significant contributions to the fields of general relativity, quantum mechanics, and black holes.

Despite being diagnosed with a rare motor neuron disease called amyotrophic lateral sclerosis (ALS) at the age of 21, Hawking continued his work and became one of the most brilliant minds of his generation.

His best-selling book, "A Brief History of Time," brought complex scientific concepts to a general audience. Stephen Hawking passed away on March 14, 2018, leaving behind a legacy that continues to inspire and influence the scientific community.

Nick Vujicic

Nick Vujicic is a motivational speaker, author, and evangelist born on December 4, 1982, in Melbourne, Australia. He was born with tetra-amelia syndrome, a rare disorder characterized by the absence of arms and legs.

Despite facing significant physical challenges, Nick Vujicic has become a source of inspiration for millions around the world. He travels globally, sharing his life story, motivational messages, and spreading a message of hope and positivity.

Nick Vujicic is the author of several books, including "Life Without Limits" and "Unstoppable." Through his foundation, Life Without Limbs, he works to support individuals with disabilities and inspire people to overcome obstacles in their lives.

Marlee Matlin

Marlee Matlin is an American actress and author, born on August 24, 1965. She is the only deaf performer to have won the Academy Award for Best Actress for her role in the film "Children of a Lesser God" (1986).

Matlin lost her hearing at the age of 18 months due to a fever, but she didn't let her deafness hinder her passion for acting. Besides her notable film achievements, she has also appeared in various television series, including "The West Wing" and "Switched at Birth."

Marlee Matlin is a prominent advocate for the deaf community and a motivational speaker. In addition to her acting career, she has authored several books, sharing her experiences and insights about living with hearing loss.
